About the Priest

Sriman Samudrala Venkatacharya Swamy is a highly respected Hindu Religious Priest with over 23 years of dedicated service across Virginia, Maryland and Washington D.C.

He has conducted Vivaha, Gruhapravesam, Satyanarayana Vratham, Seemantham, Namakaranam, Upanayanam, Rudrabhishekam and many more Vedic rituals.

He is fluent in multiple languages and explains rituals in a simple and meaningful manner for devotees of all ages.

His devotion and commitment ensure that every ceremony becomes spiritually fulfilling and memorable.
