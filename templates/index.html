About the Priest

Sriman Samudrala Venkatacharya Swamy is a highly respected Hindu Religious Priest with over 23 years of dedicated service to the Hindu community across Virginia, Maryland, and the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. Deeply rooted in the Vedic tradition, he has devoted his life to preserving and promoting the spiritual, cultural, and religious values of Sanatana Dharma.

Throughout his years of service, he has conducted a wide range of Vedic rituals including Vivaha (weddings), Gruhapravesam, Satyanarayana Vratham, Seemantham, Namakaranam, Upanayanam, Annaprasana, Rudrabhishekam, Lakshmi Puja, Ganapathi Homam, Navagraha Homam, Sudarshana Homam, Ayush Homam, and many other sacred ceremonies.

He is fluent in English, Kannada, Sanskrit, Hindi, Telugu, and Tamil, allowing him to serve a diverse community. He is known for clearly explaining the spiritual meaning and purpose of rituals, making them accessible to all devotees regardless of background.

His compassionate nature and dedication to service ensure every ceremony becomes a spiritually uplifting and meaningful experience. He welcomes everyone seeking spiritual guidance and blessings.
